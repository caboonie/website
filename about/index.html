About SaveTFP

We are a student organization that hosts fun events every Friday night! We always have lots of tasty food and provide a relaxed, entertaining social atmosphere. Our events are always free and open to the entire MIT community.

We are sponsored by the Alcohol and Other Drugs Services office. Feel free to contact us with questions or ideas at savetfp@mit.edu.

Our official motto is:

SaveTFP is committed to reducing stress and facilitating student social activities while increasing health awareness and curbing dangerous behaviors at MIT.
